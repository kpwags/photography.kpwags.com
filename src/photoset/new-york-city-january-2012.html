New York City

January 13 - 15, 2012 17 Photos

One of the only times we went to New York City and stayed outside Manhattan. We stayed in Queens which was awesome because the park nearby in Long Island City gave us amazing views of the Manhattan skyline. On Saturday we took the subway into Manhattan and ventured around Central Park and Midtown.
